So close to all that Durham has to offer, from dining to entertainment, The Heights LaSalle is a great place to call home. These luxury studio, one- and two-bedroom apartment homes boast features to suit almost any need. They start with decorator touches like 9-foot tray ceilings and double crown molding, cut pile carpeting, designer color schemes and optional hardwood flooring. They also have spacious kitchens with granite countertops and extra large breakfast bars. Select units include built-in pantries. The private balcony or patio makes a quiet spot to relax at the end of the day. Other optional features include full-size washers and dryers. Outside your luxury home, the amenities continue to impress. At the center of the courtyard area, you'll find a stunning resort-style pool with a lounge area, poolside grills and complimentary Wi-Fi service so you can stay connected while you relax. The fabulous outdoor fireplace makes a great place to socialize with neighbors or invite friends to enjoy with you. The elegant clubhouse features a full kitchen as well as lounging areas, a Cyber cafe, and conference and study rooms for your convenience. With services like valet trash and recycling at your door, valet dry cleaning and free breakfasts during the week, you'll wonder how you ever managed before moving to The Heights LaSalle.

    • Properties that may be coming to the market soon but are not yet found on a multiple listing service (MLS). They include pre-foreclosures, foreclosed homes and homes with a Make Me Move® price. Most of these properties are only found on Zillow.

      • These properties are owned by a bank or a lender who took ownership through foreclosure proceedings. They may soon be listed for sale. These properties are not found on a multiple listing service (MLS).

      • The lender initiated foreclosure proceedings on these properties because the owner(s) were in default on their loan obligations. Pre-foreclosures also include properties for which a foreclosure auction is scheduled. These properties are not found on a multiple listing service (MLS).

      • Homes with a Make Me Move® price indicate the amount the owner(s) would be willing to sell for. They are exclusive to Zillow and a great way to learn about homes before they hit the market.

    • A pending listing means a seller has accepted an offer from a buyer. In some cases, the seller will accept backup offers.
